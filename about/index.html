About Us

Shavuot EDU — Agricultural Education Platform

Who We Are

Shavuot EDU is the educational arm of the Shavuot System ecosystem, dedicated to delivering high-quality agricultural education through digital channels, podcasts, and in-person events across Venezuela and Latin America.

Our Mission

To democratize access to agricultural knowledge by connecting farmers, agronomists, researchers, and industry professionals through engaging educational content and collaborative events.

Our Vision

To become the leading agricultural education platform in Latin America, fostering a community of continuous learners who drive sustainable innovation in the agricultural sector.

What We Offer

Legume Forum — Venezuela's most innovative annual event bringing together the entire agricultural value chain for networking, education, and collaboration.

Educational Cycles — Structured learning programs covering legumes, fertilization, crop protection, and sustainable farming practices.

Video Library — A growing collection of podcast-format educational videos featuring expert guests and practical demonstrations.

Certifications — Virtual certificates awarded upon completion of cycle tests, recognizing your commitment to professional development.
